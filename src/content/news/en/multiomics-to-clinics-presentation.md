In a recent Medical Grand Rounds presentation at the Jewish General Hospital, **Dr. Brent Richards**, Professor of Medicine at McGill University, delivered an insightful talk on the integration of omics data into everyday clinical practice. 

For those who were unable to attend the hybrid event, a full video recording of the presentation is now available online.

### Educational Objectives
The presentation covers critical advancements in precision medicine, specifically focusing on:
* **Large-Scale Genomics:** How genetic data can be leveraged to improve the care and management of common diseases.
* **Proteomic Risk Identification:** How analyzing proteins helps us identify patients at elevated risk of disease before clinical onset.
* **The JGH BioPortal Program:** A comprehensive overview of the BioPortal infrastructure and how clinicians and researchers can assist with patient recruitment.
